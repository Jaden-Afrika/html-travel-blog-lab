Visit to Fire Island

Hit up Fire Island in the fall and it was super chill. Beaches were empty, breeze was crisp, and that golden sunlight was just hitting right. Walked along the shore, waves doing their thing, and honestly just vibed,felt like the island was on slow-mo. Left feeling way too relaxed. Literally felt like if "Wonder" by Shawn Mendes was a place. Grabbed some snacks, kicked back on a blanket, and just watched the waves roll in. People were few and far between, so it was easy to zone out and forget about everything else. Caught a killer sunset too—sky going pink and orange—and lowkey, it felt like time didn’t exist for a minute. Just pure fall vibes.
